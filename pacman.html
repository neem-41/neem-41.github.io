Pacman AI

Pacman is a famous video game that gained its popularity as an arcade game. The way that the game is played is that there is a board with pacman (the player), ghosts and food. The goal of the game is to eat all the food on the board. The ghosts are the opposition to the player and try to stop players from eating the food. Once all food are eaten a new board pops up and the game repeats. If the ghosts are able to stop pacman, the game is lost and pacman restarts.

Pacman is a very highly used game for algorithms and Artificial Intelligence (AI) classes. For this assignment, we used it as a way to learn different algorithms to make an AI agent. The main idea behind this assignment was to implement an algorithm known as q-learning to our pacman agent. The top down concept is that our pacman agent plays a lot of games and learns from each failure and success in terms of values for the board. Then with enough learning, it will be able to predict values for different board and play well on those.
